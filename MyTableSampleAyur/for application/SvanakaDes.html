Netradhara

    It involves mild pouring of herbal decoction through the inner corners of the eyes . Medicinal decoction is carefully poured through the inner end of the eye and let to flow through the whole eye. The process is repeated for both eyes, a number of times. Medicine enters the channels of eyes and head, washes out the dirt and at the same time reduces inflammations, if any

Indication
    Pain, itching, watering, burning sensation, and reddish discoloration of the eyes

Ingredients
    The decoction used for this is made from black raisins, liquorice, berberis,myrobalan, lodh,sandalwood and honey.

Benefits
    It cleans and removes various impurities from the eyes. The cooling effect of the herbs and healing power of honey, combined, gives a wonderful feeling to the person's eyes.
