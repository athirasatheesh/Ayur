Abhyangam

    The treatment involves a full body massage using oil. Oil of tolerable warmth, is applied to the entire body of the person lying relaxingly on the treatment table. Oil is let to absorb into the skin for 4-5 minutes. Slow massage is then given, using the whole palm and fingers. Active massage is given on the vital parts, like the sole of feet, palms, fingertips etc., where more nerve ending are concentrated. This is continued for nearly 45/50 minutes and then the person is left to rest for about 15 minutes for the oil to act.It is recommended to have one or two weeks of this oil massage, along with oral medicines like Kashayam (decoction) Lehyam (semi solid), Choornam (powder) etc.

Oils used
    Popular oils like Dhanwantharam, Ksheerabala etc. are the ones used for this massage. These oils are a blend of sesame oil and medicinal herbs like mustard,butterfly pea,eclipta, neem bark, bael, sida, spreading hog weed, ten roots, rock salt, sweet flag etc.

Benefits
    Such massages revitalize the joints and muscles and effectively relieve many inflammatory diseases. It also also helps to activate and stimulate the body nerves, helps surface the toxins inside the body and rejuvenates the persons mind.
