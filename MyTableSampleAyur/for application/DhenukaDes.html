Netratharpanam

    Retaining warm medicated ghee over the eyes is termed netratharpanam the person lies down comfortably on the wooden treatment table. Then, using black gram paste, a thick enclosure is created around the eyes in order to hold the medicated ghee. The next step is to pour warm medicated ghee through the inner corners of the eyes, sufficient enough to cover the eye lashes. Eyes are carefully opened and closed periodically to enhance the medicinal action. Warm ghee is added at intervals to ensure that the applied ghee remains in liquid state throughout the treatment. After about 15 to 20 minutes the ghee is removed and eyes are wiped with cotton.

Indication
    Short sight, cataract, blurring of vision, dry eye, glaucoma, pain in the eye, headache due to refractive error are few of the other common eye ailments treated.

Ingredients
    The medicinal herbs and components used in the ghee include tribhala,coriander,sandalwood,vitex leptadenia, liquorice, wild snake gourd, goat milk, and cow milk.

Benefits
    It relaxes the nervous system and improves the blood circulation ,reduces degeneration and relieves various ailments.
