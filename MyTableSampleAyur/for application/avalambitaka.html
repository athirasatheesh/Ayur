Gandhoosham

    Treatment involves simple gargling using warm medicinal liquid. Medicated oil, milk, or medicinal decoction is used as the gargling agent, based on the type of disease. Herbs and spices that possess cleansing, healing, and mucous fighting properties are used in these agents. The treatment begins with a light oil massage on the person's face, neck, and shoulders. This is followed by mild steaming of his face and chest. After this, the medicinal liquid is held in the person's mouth for about 4 to 5 minutes, or as long as he can tolerate, and then thrown out. This is done at least 3 or 4 times in a day. Pure herbal medicines like Kashayam (decoction) and Lehyam (semi-solid) are also taken along with the treatment and should be continued for 1 or 2 weeks for good results.

Indications
    Many ailments of the ear, nose, eye, throat, mouth canal, and head. Common complaints like sinusitis, anorexia, nausea, dryness of mouth, foul smell, Bell's palsy, mouth ulcer, teeth and gum diseases, etc. are treated.

Benefits
    The treatment removes mild toxins and clears the channels. The medicines perforate these cleared channels of the head and rectify the afflicted parts.

Ingredients
    The gargling liquid is prepared by boiling basil leaves, cardamom,myrobalan,liquorice, lodh, coleus leaves, dry ginger, berberis,Indian Gum Arabic Tree etc. in water, milk, or oil. Into this, honey and common salt is also added./p>
